## Overview
This study tested whether the Brief Course Belonging Scale (BCBS) functions equivalently for online and face-to-face college courses. Results supported partial scalar invariance, indicating the overall factor structure is comparable across contexts, though some item-level differences suggest caution when comparing latent means.
